Reporting

Share your water scarcity issues directly through the app. Your reports will be addressed by the concerned authorities or supported by local NGOs and donations.

Community Support

Join efforts to tackle water scarcity by submitting your problem. Get solutions from water experts, and even raise donations to solve the problem in your area.

Expert Solutions

Benefit from professional consultations, and allow local authorities and organizations to help resolve your water issues effectively.

Our Focus

Our focus is to provide quick and sustainable solutions to water scarcity problems by facilitating reporting, expert consultations, and community-driven donations.
